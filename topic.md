### **1. Phage Genome Analysis**

1. **Hybrid Assembly Benchmarking for Nanopore Phage Data**
   Develop an optimized hybrid genome assembly workflow that combines Nanopore long reads and Illumina short reads for resolving phage genome mosaicism and terminal redundancy.

2. **Automated Annotation of Mobile Genetic Elements in Phage Genomes**
   Create a deep learning–based pipeline to detect and annotate lysogeny modules, integrases, and antimicrobial resistance cassettes in newly sequenced phage genomes.

---

### **2. Comparative Phage Genomics**

3. **Pan-Genome Evolution Across Host-Restricted Phages**
   Conduct comparative pan-genome analysis of phages infecting different *Staphylococcus aureus* lineages to uncover mechanisms of host adaptation and immune evasion.

4. **Horizontal Gene Transfer Mapping Among Phage Clades**
   Use graph-based clustering and phylogenomic profiling to map horizontal gene transfer events between environmental and clinical phage populations.

---

### **3. In Silico Host Range Prediction**

5. **Transformer-Based Prediction of Phage–Host Interactions**
   Build a large-scale transformer model trained on CRISPR spacer and receptor-binding domain data to predict host range with species-level precision.

6. **Integrative Phage–Host Interaction Network Reconstruction**
   Combine genomic features, prophage signals, and metagenomic co-occurrence networks to predict and validate host–phage associations at the ecosystem scale.

---

### **4. Phage Metagenomics**

7. **Unsupervised Binning of Phage Genomes in Complex Viromes**
   Design an unsupervised deep clustering model (e.g., variational autoencoder) for reconstructing complete phage genomes from metavirome datasets.

8. **Functional Metagenomics of Antibiotic Resistance Phages**
   Analyze resistome-carrying phages in hospital wastewater metagenomes to understand phage-mediated gene mobilization across bacterial communities.

---

### **5. Phage Cocktail Optimization**

9. **Combinatorial Optimization of Phage Cocktails Using AI-Driven Scoring**
   Develop a reinforcement learning framework that predicts optimal phage combinations based on host range overlap, mutation escape probability, and synergy assays.

10. **Modeling Phage Cocktail Dynamics in Polymicrobial Infections**
    Create a systems biology model simulating multi-phage–multi-host interactions to optimize dosing strategies against mixed MDR pathogen populations.
